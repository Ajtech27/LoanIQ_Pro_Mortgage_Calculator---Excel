 ### LoanIQ Pro — Loan & Mortgage Calculator
**Domain:** Personal Finance & Financial Modeling

A professional loan and mortgage analysis tool covering everything a borrower or financial advisor needs before signing — monthly payment, total cost, amortization schedule, lender comparison, and early repayment savings. Zero formula errors across 2,630 formulas.

**5 Sheets:** Loan Calculator · Loan Comparison · Early Repayment · Amortization · Dashboard

**Key features:**
- Monthly payment, total interest, total cost, and payoff date from 4 inputs
- Affordability checker against the 28% income guideline
- 3-lender side-by-side comparison with effective APR and best-deal verdict
- Early repayment savings modeled across 4 overpayment scenarios simultaneously
- Full 360-month amortization with principal, interest, equity, and cumulative interest

**Tools:** Excel · PMT · NPER · RATE · IFERROR · ColorScale Conditional Formatting
